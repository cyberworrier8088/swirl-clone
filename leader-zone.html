How do I run a Swirl Workshop?

Step 1 Prepare!

A few days before your workshop, you're going to want to prepare! Here's some suggestions:

  • Familiarize yourself with what you're covering in the workshop! We have resources for you and your club members. Make sure you know the requirements for a Swirl website.
  • You may want to make slides (or use use!) and make a demo website. It can be helpful when explaining things to participants.
  • Have all the necessary materials ready! This can include: laptops, internet, GitHub access, chargers, and (like this website planner).
  • Fill out this form to create your workshop once you're ready. This step is required to ship and get your ice cream!
